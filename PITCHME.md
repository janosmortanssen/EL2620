### Linear Systems

**Definition:** Let $M$ be a signal
space. The system $S:M\rightarrow M$ is linear if for all $u,v\in M$ and $\alpha\in \mathbf{R}$

$$
S(\alpha u) = \alpha S(u)\\
S(u+v)=S(u)+S(v)
$$
